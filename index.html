Welcome to Shuwaki Worxpace

Shuwaki Worxpace® is a business management software developed by Shuwaki Ibrahim and Digital Worxpaces Nig. Ltd. It features a combined point of sale, inventory management, customer/credit management, and accounting workloads. The cornerstone of Shuwaki Worxpace® is compact workspaces in single dashboards and ease of workflow. This makes working with Shuwaki Worxpace® easy without compromise on basic business requirements of security and privacy.
